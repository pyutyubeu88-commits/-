About S&H Dual Waxing
Who we are · What makes us different
Why is it called "Dual" Waxing?
Most waxing studios offer one method and ask you to adapt to it. We believe your skin deserves better. S&H Dual Waxing is the only studio in Apgujeong where you choose between hard waxing (firm, fast, ideal for sensitive skin) and sugaring (natural sugar paste, gentler on delicate areas) — based on what works for your skin, not ours. This is what our clients love most, and what keeps them coming back.
